<h2>{% include i18n-text.html en="What is Capstone Design at SUSTech?" zh="什么是南科大的综合设计课程？" %}</h2>

<div class="lang-en" markdown="1">
The Capstone Design (Comprehensive Design) project was inspired by the undergraduate engineering education model in the United States, and its educational approach was later discussed in a Chinese higher-education journal. My team and I contributed one of the featured case studies.
</div>

<div class="lang-zh" markdown="1">
综合设计（Capstone Design）课程借鉴了美国本科工程教育中的毕业设计模式，相关教学思路后来也发表在中文高等教育期刊上。我们团队的项目被作为案例之一收录其中。
</div>

<h3 class="publication-section-heading">{% include i18n-text.html en="Abstract" zh="摘要" %}</h3>
<div class="lang-en">
  <p>This paper discusses various models of emerging engineering talent training both at home and abroad, and summarizes the common characteristics of these models. In the context of deepening engineering-education reform and implementing Emerging Engineering Education in China, the College of Engineering of the Southern University of Science and Technology launched a “Comprehensive Design” course as an integral part of its new engineering curriculum. The course offers special-topic lectures and dual mentorship for industry projects in order to cultivate students' problem-solving ability, teamwork, engineering ethics, and international perspective, while also strengthening collaboration between the university and industry.</p>
</div>

<div class="lang-zh">
  <p>本文梳理了国内外新工科人才培养模式的典型路径，并总结其共性特征。在深化工程教育改革、推进新工科建设的背景下，南方科技大学工学院开设了“综合设计”课程，作为新工科体系中的关键实践环节。课程通过专题讲座、企业项目和双导师制，系统培养学生的问题解决能力、团队协作精神、工程伦理意识与国际化视野，同时也进一步强化了学校与产业之间的联系。</p>
</div>
